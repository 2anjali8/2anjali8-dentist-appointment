WHAT WE DO?

Preventive Dentistry

We provide several treatments to strengthen and support your teeth and gums. Our services include dental exams, cleaning, fillings, sealants, tooth extractions. We offer pediatric dental services for kids

Cosmetic Dentistry

We offer many cosmetic options to enhance your smile. Veneers are thin covers that are placed over the front of a tooth that may be chipped, discolored or needs an uneven space corrected. We provide teeth whitening and have Invisalign to help restore your youthful smile.

Tooth Replacement

A dental crown, or dental cap, is a type of dental restoration which completely caps or encircles a tooth or dental implant. We do dentures, root canals and gum disease treatments and emergency dental when you need it. We are here to help.
